"Wiki for phpWebSite":http://github.com/meisteg/wiki brings the power and convenience of a wiki to "phpWebSite":http://phpwebsite.appstate.edu. This minor bugfix release fixes issues with the anchor and TOC links. phpWebSite 1.2.0 or later is required.

With the release, the Wiki module project is now "hosted on GitHub":http://github.com/meisteg/wiki/downloads.

If you have any questions or comments about the Wiki module, "please submit a ticket":http://github.com/meisteg/wiki/issues on the project site.

Enjoy the new version!
